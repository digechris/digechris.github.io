My IT ServiceDesk Experience


Whenever I was not working with Adham, I would work on Matt's team on Asset Management. The goal of asset management was the tracking of all of Oldcastle APG's hardware. The main goal at the moment was to track the laptops. We would go through and verify SCCM data and match it up with the user and transfer this information over to the new procurement sheet.

This may seem simple, but up until now there have been a large portion of errors.
Once everything was confirmed, I would input tags into SNOW and assign a new hardware asset (Make, Model, etc) to that specific user. (Assigned to, Location, etc).
